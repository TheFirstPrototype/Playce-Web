Say Hello to Playce

Playce is an early childcare centre management tool with a focus on structured educational outcomes and centre profitability. This involve features that assist with managing schedules, attendance, curriculum planning, parent communication, and even financial aspects of running a childcare business.


By providing a comprehensive platform that combines educational and business management tools, our company aims to help childcare providers optimize their operations.
